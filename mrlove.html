Background setting

The player is operating a film and television company that is going to be bankrupt. There is a kind of power called 'Evol' in this city, so the player plans to start a television production program called 'Discovering miracles'. This program focuses on how Evol influences ordinary people's life. During the process of producing the program, the player will meet the 4 main characters and start her adventure.
